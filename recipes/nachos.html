Nachos

Nothing is better than a declicious plate of homemade nachos. This is a recipe both delicious and easy to make. You can easily customize the dish as well, by changing up the variety of meat used, choosing different type of cheese and whatever toppings catch your fancy.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Shredded Mexican Cheese
  • Ground Chicken
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Black Beans
  • Onion
  • Green Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Sour Cream

Directions

  1. Prehead oven to 350F
  2. While oven prheating, dice the onion and pepper and start sauteing in pan with olive oil on medium high heat
  3. Add the ground chicken to pan and start browning, adding the taco seasoning
  4. On a baking sheet, spread out the tortilla chips
  5. Spread half the cheese evenly on top of the tortilla chips
  6. Drain the can of black beans, rinse and spread on top of cheese
  7. Drain oil from pan and spoon the meat, peppers & onion on top of chips
  8. Add remaining cheese on top of the chips
  9. Dice tomatoes and place on top of chips
  10. Place in oven and bake for 10-12 minutes
  11. Let cool for 3 minutes, and serve with sour cream
