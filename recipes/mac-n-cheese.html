Macaroni & Cheese

Macaroni and cheese, an American classic! Mac and cheese was a staple for me and many kids growing up due to how tasty it is and how easy it is for kids to make when their parents weren't able to cook for them. It is easy to make macroni and cheese that is as good or better than the Kraft version which is most popular.

Ingredients

  • Box of Kraft Macaroni and Cheese
  • Broccoli
  • Hot Dog
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Boil macaroni in pot of water, add broccoli 5 mins before completion
  2. Heat up hot dog in microwave for 30 seconds and then chip into half inch portions
  3. Drain macaroni and broccoli
  4. Add butter, milk, hot dog, and cheese powder from box to drained pot with macaroni
  5. Stir until blended
  6. Add shredded cheese to taste and stir in until blended
