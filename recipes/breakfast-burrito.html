Breakfast Burrito

Breakfast burritos are easy to make and delicious to eat. They are supremely customizable so that no matter what taste preferences you are dealing with, you will be able to tailor the burrito to those specifications.

Ingredients

  • 8-inch Tortillas
  • bacon
  • eggs
  • shredded cheese
  • spinach
  • salsa
  • milk (optional)

Directions

  1. Fry bacon in pan until desired crispness and then remove and place on plate with paper towels to drain some grease.
  2. Break eggs into bowl and whip until blended. You can add the optional milk if you would prefer fluffier eggs.
  3. Drain excess bacon grease from pan leaving some for eggs.
  4. Add spinach to pan and heat up until hot and then add eggs and scramble them together until eggs are cooked and then remove from heat.
  5. Heat up tortilla in separate pan.
  6. Place tortilla on plate, add two slices bacon, eggs, and top with shredded cheese and salsa to taste. Then wrap tortilla around everything.
  7. Enjoy!
